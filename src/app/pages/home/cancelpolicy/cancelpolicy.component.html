Cancellation Policy of Black Brusa

What is Black Brusa Cancellation Policy?

You can cancel an order through the Black Brusa website/M-site until it has been packed at our warehouse. This includes items purchased on sale also. Any amount paid will be credited into the same payment mode using which the payment was made. Still, if you wish to cancel your order after the order is packed, reach out to our Customer Care team at customercare@blackbrusa.com or on +91 99999 00000.
