Shipping Policy of Black Brusa

How long will it take for my order to reach me?

We process and ship every order within 48-72 business hours of receiving it. Your order will get delivered within 5-7 business days depending on your location in India. In some cases, it may take longer to receive the shipment depending on the accessibility of your location.

What delivery methods do you use?

We use leading courier companies such as Ecom Express for shipment in India.

What should be done if my package has been opened or tampered with?

If your package has been opened or tampered with, we request you to not accept the shipment and immediately inform our customer care team via phone at +91-8178566024 / customercare@blackbrusa.com. Kindly note, any intimation after 24 hours of the attempted delivery, will not be considered.
