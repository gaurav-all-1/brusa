Return & Exchange

Black Brusa’s 15-days returns and exchange policy gives you an option to return or exchange items purchased from the Black Brusa website/m-site for any reason within 15 days of receipt of the item. We only ask that you don't use the product and preserve its original condition, tags and packaging. You are welcome to try on a product but please take adequate measures to preserve its condition.

Kindly note, all exchanges are subject to stock availability. Please also note that we only offer size exchanges. If you wish to exchange your item for an alternate product, we suggest that you return it to obtain store credit and purchase the new item separately.

The following EXCEPTIONS and RULES apply to our 15-days Return & Exchange Policy:

  1. 1.

    All items to be returned or exchanged must be unused and should be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact (e.g. shoes must be packed in the original shoe box).

  2. 2.

    We only accept returns for products purchased directly from Black Brusa’s website, mobile site. Any return request for products purchased from our indirect online and offline distributors will be declined by us.

  3. 3.

    Please note that we do not refund the shipping or COD charges incurred by you.

  4. 4.

    The exchange policy allows for only size exchanges.

  5. 5.

    If you self-ship your return/exchange, kindly pack the items securely to prevent any loss or damage during transit. For all self-shipped returns, we recommend you use a reliable courier service. A fixed amount of Rs. 100 will be reimbursed subject to the return having met our Returns and Exchange Policy

  6. 6.

    Please check the items, type of return/exchange, and other related details carefully. Our courier partner will pick up only the items mentioned at the time of submitting the return/exchange request by you

  7. 7.

    In case the items handed over to the courier person don't match the details of the items mentioned in the return/exchange request, we will conduct an internal probe and get back to you accordingly

  8. 8.

    If you self-ship any product without officially informing us through our return/exchange process, we will not do the process of return

How do I place an exchange request on Black Brusa?

If you want to exchange products purchased from Black Brusa, please follow the below-mentioned steps:

  2. 2.

    We offer a pick-up facility in select locations basis our courier serviceability. If you select to schedule a pick-up, please place the product(s) in a packet. The product(s) must be unused, unwashed and all the tags should be intact. We will pick up the exchange within 1 - 3 business days from the exchange request placement date.

What should I do if I receive a damaged/defective/incomplete/wrong order from Black Brusa?

For any defective/ damaged/incomplete/wrong order delivered, we request you to inform us within 48 hours of receiving the order with the images of the product received. You can do so by clicking on 'Return’ under the ‘My Orders’ section of the Black Brusa Website/M-site and follow the instructions to upload the images. Please keep in mind the following–

  1. 1.

    You need to upload a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 pictures

  2. 2.

    Kindly ensure the images are clear and the damaged/defective/missing part of the outfit is highlighted in red

  3. 3.

    You must send us an image of the original labels and tags intact along with the invoice.

In case you face any technical difficulties in following this process, kindly get in touch with us on customercare@blackbrusa.com or call us on +91- We will then investigate the case and reply within 48 hours with the best available resolution. Any such complaint raised after 48 hours of receiving the order may not be considered.

Why did the pick-up of my product fail?

We make three attempts to pick up the item. If the item is not picked up on the two attempt, the pickup request will be marked as failed

How do I return/exchange multiple products from a single order?

  1. 1.

    If you are returning/exchanging multiple products from a single order together, we will generate a single Return ID/Exchange ID for all products and you will receive an e-mail and SMS confirmation for the same

  2. 2.

    If you wish to return/exchange another product from a single order at a later date, still within the 15-days return policy, we will accept your return/exchange and arrange a reverse pick-up for the second time. The subsequent returns/exchanges for the same order will be self-shipped by you and no reimbursement for the same will be provided by us in any form.

How long will it take for me to receive the refund of my returned product?

  1. 1.

    If your return order is picked up by us, the refund process will be initiated within 24-48 business hours of the pickup, and a refund confirmation email and SMS will be sent to you. The refund amount will reflect in your source account in the next 5- 7 business days. In case the refund is to be made in the form Black Brusa Store Credits, the amount will reflect in 24-48 business hours.

  2. 2.

    If you self-ship the return order, the product(s) will first go through a quality check at our end. Once it clears the check, the refund process will be initiated within 24-48 business hours and the amount will reflect in your source account in the next 5- 7 business days. If the refund is to be made in the form of Black Brusa Store Credits, the amount will reflect in 24-48 business hours. In case the product does not meet our quality check, we will not be able to process the refund

  3. 3.

    In case of any refund discrepancies, Black Brusa may at its sole discretion, request you to share with us a screenshot of your bank/credit card/wallet statement.
